About

I teach cornet and trumpet to people of all ages from beginner level all the way up to intermediate. I will tailor your session to your tastes and what you want to achieve out of playing. I can help you through ABRSM grades too if you wish. I am a mobile tutor and have an up to date CRB check.

Who I Am

My name is Bridget. I am 27 years old. I have always been very musical, and enjoyed playing the cornet since the age of ten. I have played in several brass bands growing up and I also enjoy playing the trumpet and regularly attend jazz sessions in Sheffield. I set up Simply Music because I am passionate about making music and want to inspire others to pick up an instrument and have a go at playing!
